About Autopatch

Autopatch is an innovative solution for detecting and addressing potholes on roads. It enables users to lodge complaints regarding road damages easily, ensuring that authorities can swiftly attend to the issue and improve road safety.

Key Features

  • Real-time pothole detection and reporting
  • User-friendly complaint submission system
  • Automated notifications for new complaints
  • Admin dashboard for monitoring and resolving complaints
  • Track the status of each reported complaint

Our Mission

At Autopatch, our mission is to enhance road safety by providing a platform where citizens can report road issues quickly, allowing for timely repairs and safer driving conditions for everyone.
